If using these data please cite:

Schneider L, Smerdon JE, Pretis F, Hartl-Meier C, Esper J (accepted) A new archive of large volcanic events over the past millennium derived from reconstructed summer temperatures. Environmental Research Letters.


File descriptions:


eventlistXXX.csv

- detection results produced with the impulse indicator saturation method in OxMetrics
- model setup as explained in the main paper
- XXX refers to the respective temperature record


temperature_records.csv

- Annually resolved summer temperature records for the Northern Hemisphere mid- and highlatitudes from SCH15, WIL16 and 5 PMIP3 model simulations
- for the seasonal window and the spatial coverage of each proxy- and model timeseries as well as for their abbreviations see main paper
- the original reconstruction for Northern Hemisphere summer temperatures published by Schneider et al. (2015) can be accessed here: https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/paleo-search/study/18875
- the original reconstruction for Northern Hemisphere summer temperatures published by Wilson et al. (2016) can be accessed here: https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/paleo-search/study/19743
- STO16 has not been made publicly available from the original authors
- data given as anomalies w.r.t. 1961-1990
